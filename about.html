ABOUT

I am Agatha. 29 years old. Born in Brazil.

From 19 to 21 years old I have presented myself as a journalist and its many role variations. I have been an intern, a reporter, a photographer when needed, and a news editor for a newspaper in Brazil. Everything changed when in 2014 I decided to give my life a good shake. I left all my certainties, stability, and the pleasure of having friends and family one bus away to move to the U.S.

Since 2014 I have been discovering new passions, skills, necessities, and most importantly: the ability to live many lives in just one.

In the States, I've been working and learning enough to be a marketing professional. I have served as a freelancer, an in-house marketing coordinator. For 2+ years I worked as a marketing associate in a digital marketing agency in Boston (MA). Now I spend most of my day in my full-time job working with marketing automation in the financial field.

uring my spare time I illustrate digitally and traditionally, as you can see here on page [Work]. Since the beginning of 2022 I have been learning how to code, as you can see on these website pages that I have created from scratch.
